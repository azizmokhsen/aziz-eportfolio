About Me

My passion for cooking began at a young age, helping my mother in the kitchen and admiring my grandmother’s delicious home-cooked meals. What started as a simple hobby gradually grew into a lifelong passion. I pursued my culinary education at IBTE, which laid the foundation for my journey in the culinary world. Later, I earned my Culinary Degree from KDU University in Malaysia and gained valuable experience working at the Marriott Hotel in Qatar. Along the way, I explored diverse cuisines and cooking techniques, continuously inspired by the people, cultures, and food that surrounded me throughout my life. Today, I’m proud to be an instructor, sharing my knowledge and love for the culinary arts with aspiring chefs.
